About Me

Clinical Nutritionist & Author

I'm a clinical Nutritionist and author focused on chronic health and sustainable weight management. I help individuals and families build realistic, lasting habits through evidence-based nutrition and a family-centred approach.

My writing blends practical guidance with a thoughtful, reflective tone. It's grounded in lived experience, gently questioning the norms and encouraging deeper thinking about how we live, eat, and care for ourselves and our families.

As a mum, I bring both professional insight and everyday experience to what I do — walking alongside others with empathy, depth, and purpose.

My Approach

Six Pillars of Lifestyle Medicine

A whole-person approach that helps address the root causes of chronic conditions, not just the symptoms.

🥦

Whole-Food Nutrition

Nourishing the body with whole, plant-rich foods that support long-term health and vitality.

🏃

Movement

Incorporating enjoyable, sustainable physical activity into daily life for every body.

🌙

Restorative Sleep

Prioritising quality sleep as a foundation for healing, energy, and mental clarity.

🧘

Stress Management

Building resilience through mindful practices that calm the nervous system.

🤝

Healthy Relationships

Fostering meaningful connections that support emotional and physical wellbeing.

🚫

Reducing Harmful Substances

Minimising exposure to substances that undermine health and slow recovery.

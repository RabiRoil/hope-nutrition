About the Channel

Learning Through Play & Story

Hope Nutrition Kids is a creative YouTube channel by Ramsina designed to make nutrition education fun, accessible, and meaningful for young learners. Through engaging stories, colourful art, hand puppets, and clay animation, children discover the joy of healthy eating while building empathy and life skills.

Each episode is crafted with care — blending gentle moral lessons with nutrition knowledge in an age-appropriate, screen-time-worthy format that parents can feel good about.

For Parents

Screen Time You Can Feel Good About

Hope Nutrition Kids is designed to be the kind of screen time parents can feel genuinely good about. Every episode is created by a clinical nutritionist and mum who understands the challenge of teaching healthy habits to little ones.

The channel is ad-free, age-appropriate, and intentionally paced — perfect for toddlers and young children who are just beginning to understand the world of food.
